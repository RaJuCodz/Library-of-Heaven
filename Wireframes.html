Chapter 3

Teacher Zhang's Secret

The morning sun cast long shadows across the Academy courtyard as Zhang Xuan made his way to the teaching hall. Three years he had spent here, yet today felt different — the air itself seemed to hum with invisible potential.

He paused before the carved doors, noticing the faint golden script that had never been visible before. His new ability — the Library of Heaven's Path — pulsed like a heartbeat in his chest, eager to uncover the secrets woven into every stone of this ancient place.

"The heavens reveal their truths only to those who seek without agenda," whispered the inscription. Zhang Xuan smiled. For the first time in his failed scholar's life, he understood what that meant.

The library shimmered at the edge of his consciousness: a vast, luminous palace with shelves that reached into infinity. Every technique ever practiced within these walls had left its imprint, and all their flaws lay bare to his gaze.

He entered the teaching hall and faced his students — noble-born children who had mocked him endlessly. Today, that would change.

Today, the Library of Heaven would speak through him.
